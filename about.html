About GlobalWorks Cybersecurity

GlobalWorks Cybersecurity is an international security firm focused on protecting individuals, businesses, and institutions from evolving cyber threats. With roots in both the United States and the Philippines, we merge global expertise with local responsiveness. Our mission is to deliver reliable, innovative, and accessible digital security solutions to clients of all sizes.

From our U.S. base, we deploy enterprise-grade systems to corporations and government partners. Meanwhile, our outreach in the Philippines empowers small businesses, students, and emerging tech leaders through cybersecurity training and consulting.

Our services include network defense, ethical hacking, incident response, data recovery, cloud protection, and more. We are committed to building a safer digital world—one device, one system, one person at a time.
